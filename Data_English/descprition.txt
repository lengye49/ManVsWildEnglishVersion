Lost Land, a text adventure game designed by skygames.

In this game, you will be a man who entered a land with many magical creatures

Build your workshop and altar, capture powerful creatures like dragons, protect your treasure from thieves, arm yourself with powerful weapons, explore the mysterious dungeon, enter the space vortex and finally get back to where you belong.

Just relax, it's not that dangerous. You can also keep a pet like unicorn, make wine and enjoy farming. Take your time.
